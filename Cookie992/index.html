Cookie Policy

How TapBlast Arena uses cookies to enhance your gaming experience

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you browse websites. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and give website owners information about how users interact with their sites.

Cookies allow websites to remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and in some cases, show you relevant advertising based on your browsing behavior. They're an important part of the modern web experience.

How TapBlast Arena Uses Cookies

At TapBlast Arena, we use cookies for various purposes to enhance your experience on our website and with our game. Here's how we use different types of cookies:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation, access to secure areas, and game loading. The website cannot function properly without these cookies, and they can only be disabled by changing your browser settings.

  • User session management
  • Security features
  • Load balancing functionality
  • Basic website operations

Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your choices and settings to provide enhanced, personalized features. They are used to remember your preferences (such as language, region, or username) and settings to make your experience better and more tailored to your interests.

  • Language preferences
  • Game settings and configurations
  • User interface customizations
  • Volume settings

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website and game by collecting and reporting information anonymously. The data collected helps us improve our website, game mechanics, and user experience based on user behavior patterns.

  • Understanding which game features are most popular
  • Tracking how users navigate through our website
  • Identifying areas where users may experience difficulties
  • Measuring the effectiveness of our game design and content

Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your browsing habits to enable us to show advertising which is more likely to be of interest to you. They are also used to limit the number of times you see an advertisement and help measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

  • Displaying relevant game offers and features
  • Measuring ad campaign effectiveness
  • Limiting ad frequency
  • Personalizing marketing messages

Third-Party Cookies

Some of our pages display content from external providers, like YouTube, Facebook, or Twitter. To view this third-party content, you first have to accept their specific terms and conditions. This includes their cookie policies, which we have no control over.

If you do not view this content, no third-party cookies are installed on your device.

Cookie Management

You can manage your cookie preferences in several ways. We provide a cookie consent banner when you first visit our site, where you can choose which categories of cookies you accept or reject.

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to manage your cookie preferences. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, delete cookies, or alert you when cookies are being sent. Data Protection and Privacy

The data we collect via cookies is used in accordance with our Privacy Policy. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected and that any information we collect through cookies is handled securely.

We do not share your personal information collected through cookies with third parties except as described in our Privacy Policy. The information may be used to improve our game, website, and services and to provide personalized experiences.

Data Retention

Different cookies have different lifespans. Some expire at the end of your web session while others may persist for years. You can see the expiration date of each cookie through your browser settings. We regularly review and update our cookie policy to ensure it accurately represents our use of cookies.
